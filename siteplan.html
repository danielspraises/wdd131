Website Planning Document

Site Name

Temple Picture Album

This name represents a website showcasing LDS temples around the world with photos, location info, dedication dates, and sizes.

Optional domain: temple-album.org

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for exploring LDS temples through an interactive photo album. Users can view temple details, filter temples by age or size, and learn more about their history and locations.

Scenarios

  • Which temples were dedicated after the year 2000?
  • What are the largest temples in terms of area?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Dark Green (#006d2c) – headings, logo, navigation links
  • Secondary Color: White (#ffffff) – page background, card backgrounds
  • Accent Color: Light Green (#a1d99b) – highlights, buttons, hover effects
  • Text Color: Dark Gray (#333333) – paragraph and card text

Typography

  • Roboto: body text, paragraphs, list items
  • Open Sans: headings (H1-H3), logo
